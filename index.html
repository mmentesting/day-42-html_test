Walkways

genre: Alternative Metal

BIO:

Walkways is an alternative metal band based in Tel Aviv, Israel.
The band is driven with a goal to keep sane and evolve into
more humane and moral people while writing their music -
the soundtrack of the highs and falls of their lives.
